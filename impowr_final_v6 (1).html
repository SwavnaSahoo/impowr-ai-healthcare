The challenge
What IMPOWR needs to solve

Continual Care Solutions operates IMPOWR across 13,000+ users and 240,000 lives. The brief was to design AI-enhanced analytics that stay compliant, explainable, and clinically usable under HIPAA constraints.

Building compliant AI on regulated data

How can AI-enabled reporting run on Azure against relational care data without model training on proprietary or patient-identifiable records?

Producing measurable, real-world value

Which analytics capabilities improve compliance response times, staffing decisions, and intervention outcomes for high-risk clients?

Our answer is a three-layer design: deterministic privacy controls, an expert-defined Bayesian risk engine, and a rulebook-governed explanation layer with role-based access.

Market context
Who is in this space

Most platforms deliver scheduling and compliance workflows. The strategic gap is explainable per-client risk scoring without PHI model training.

Birdie / Access Evo / Nourish

Strong operational tooling, limited predictive safeguarding intelligence at individual-client level.

WellSky / AlayaCare

Broader North American footprint with analytics capabilities, but differentiated PHI-safe architecture remains an opportunity.

Differentiator: IMPOWR combines zero-PHI training boundaries, explainable Bayesian scoring, NLP safeguarding alerts, and SDoH-weighted logic in one auditable loop.
